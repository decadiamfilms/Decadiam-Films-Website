Short Film

Stuck In a Memory

A raw and intimate portrait of love, loss, and self-discovery following Xaden as he navigates the complexities of unresolved feelings.

Award Winner

Synopsis

Stuck In A Memory follows Xaden, a young man caught in the emotional aftermath of a broken relationship, as he struggles to let go of the past and navigate the complexities of his unresolved feelings for his ex-girlfriend, Taylor.

Through a mix of present-day interactions and fragmented flashbacks, the film paints a raw and intimate portrait of love, loss, and self-discovery. Haunted by moments of joy and pain, Xaden's journey unfolds against the backdrop of awkward reunions, heartfelt confessions, and bittersweet memories that blur the line between longing and acceptance.

With a poignant exploration of the emotional weight of heartbreak, Stuck In A Memory captures the universal struggle of moving on while holding on.

About the Film

Director's Vision

"Stuck In a Memory delves into the universal experience of heartbreak and the struggle to move forward while being anchored to the past. Through Xaden's journey, we explore how memory can become both a refuge and a prison, shaping our present through fragments of what once was."

Production Approach

The film employs a non-linear narrative structure, weaving between past and present to mirror the fragmented nature of memory and emotion. Intimate cinematography and naturalistic performances create an authentic portrayal of modern relationships.

Themes

  • Love & Loss: The complexity of unresolved romantic relationships
  • Memory & Time: How past experiences shape present reality
  • Self-Discovery: Finding identity beyond relationship definitions
  • Acceptance: The journey from longing to letting go
