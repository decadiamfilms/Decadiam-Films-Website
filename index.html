Crafting Engaging Stories

Founded in 2022 by Liam Budai, Decadiam Films is a film production company driven by a deep love for storytelling. With a focus on fiction, we bring compelling narratives to life through a diverse range of short films, with plans to expand into feature films in the future.

At Decadiam Films, we believe in the power of cinema to inspire, challenge, and transport audiences to new worlds. Our passion for filmmaking fuels every project we undertake, from intimate character-driven stories to bold, imaginative visions.

Join us as we continue to push creative boundaries and shape the future of storytelling, one film at a time.
